About Us

Founded on the principle that wearing KAWS isn't just about putting on a shirt, but embracing a lifestyle.

KAWS, founded by artist Brian Donnelly, began as a graffiti artist in NYC in the 1990s. Transitioning to fine art in the early 2000s, KAWS' distinctive style—featuring cartoonish characters with X-ed out eyes—gained global recognition. He expanded into various mediums, collaborating with brands like Nike and Dior. Today, his artwork is highly coveted, fetching high prices and influencing contemporary culture worldwide.
